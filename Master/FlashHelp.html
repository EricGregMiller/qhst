Flash Help

The videos on our site are streamed through an Adobe Flash player. If they do not appear or do not play correctly it is probably because you have a broken or old Flash player on your machine. The best fix is to uninstall your old player and install the latest Adobe Flash player.

Follow these steps to install the latest Flash player.

  1. Uninstall Flash. Follow Adobe’s instructions here: http://www.adobe.com/cfusion/knowledgebase/index.cfm?id=tn_14157
  2. Install the new Flash. Follow Adobe’s instructions here: http://www.adobe.com/shockwave/download/download.cgi?P1_Prod_Version=ShockwaveFlash

If the instructions above don’t resolve the issue please try the following:

  1. Temporarily disable popup blocking software installed on your computer to test.
  2. Allow theology.edu as a trusted site in your Firewall software.
  3. Ensure other applications like Quicktime, iTunes, Real Player, or Windows Media Player aren’t set as the default streaming application, as this might affect the video player.

The above instructions were adapter from YouTube’s help site. YouTube suggests rebooting after uninstalling your old flash but Adobe never mentions it and it was not needed in a test done by the QHST administrator. Feel free to contact the QHST web administrator with any comments or questions.
