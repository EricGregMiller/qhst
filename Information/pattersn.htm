Don R. Patterson

       Don Patterson is the Senior Pastor of Quartz Hill Community Church and President of Quartz Hill School of Theology. He has pastored and ministered to churches in California and Arkansas for the past twenty-five years. He holds a BA from California Baptist University in Riverside and an MDiv from Golden Gate Baptist Theological Seminary in Mill Valley, California. Cartooning, novel writing, and back road "exploring" take up his precious moments of free time. Currently he lives in Quartz Hill with his wife Karen, an elementary teacher at Desert Christian School. They have two adult sons.
