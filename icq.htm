ICQ
(I Seek You)

For online chats and discussions with faculty and other students, you must download and install the latest version of ICQ. This will allow you to have real time discussions with people all over the world.

To get ICQ, just click on the link below and then follow the instructions on ICQ's site.

Times for scheduled chats will be posted on the Scheduled Chats Page or will be agreed to independently by students and/or faculty, with times distributed by email.
