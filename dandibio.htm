Biography of Dandi Moyers

       Dandi Moyers has an outstanding singing voice, practices Kung Fu, works part time, and is a mother and wife all the time. As a student, she has taken classes in literature at Antelope Valley College in Lancaster, California, and in Bible, Theology and Hebrew at Quartz Hill School of Theology in Quartz Hill, California. She has contributed several articles and poems to the Quartz Hill Journal of Theology and has an abiding interest in finding the truth, no matter where it might lead. She also edits the novels of R.P. Nettelhorst.
       She is married to Dennis Moyers, an IT professional. Together, they are active in a small start-up church. They have two children, a girl named Kaila and a boy named Keaton. Two friendly dogs share their house with them in Colorado.
