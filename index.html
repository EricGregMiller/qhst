Quartz Hill School of Theology is a free and open educational resource designed to train people for more effective ministry, both in and out of the church. Our mission is to advance knowledge and serve the world in the 21st century. Our courses cover a wide range of subjects related to the Bible and theology.

Quartz Hill School of Theology is a ministry of Quartz Hill Community Church, a Baptist congregation in the High Desert of Southern California which is associated with the Southern Baptist Convention.

Our Recent Publications
Quartz Hill Publishing House is the publishing arm of Quartz Hill School of Theology. Quartz Hill Publishing House publishes reprints, nonfiction, and fiction. We have reprinted Edward Young's Night Thoughts and are in the process of producing a complete commentary series on the Bible written by our professor emeritus Dr. Jim West.

Quartz Hill Publishing House is a small academic press. Our books are print on demand. We are always open to submissions.
