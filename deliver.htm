Deliverance

by
Iris Houdyshell
My fears are conquered, my pain has gone.
Ancestral boundaries have been destroyed.
Jesus, mighty one, my Lord and King has finished
My deliverance.

Every dark corner has been revealed.
Jesus' glorious light shines all around
Those who cowered in the powerless
strongholds of the enemy have been removed in
My deliverance

To know you Father, for this I was made
Now all Hell's minions (my inheritance) are banished,
And I will sing and give highest praise to you for
My deliverance.
