I. Soteriology: The Doctrine of Salvation

Please read Parts Six and Ten of Erickson's Christian Theology, pages 561-658 and 887-1022.

Please read the following lectures:

Part I: Introduction, salvation in the Old Testament, the sacrificial system, and the age of accountability.
Part II: The Nature of the Gospel.
Part III: Important terms: reconciliation, propitiation, redemption, faith, repentance, justification, sanctification, prayer, and eternal security. Also a summary of Armenianism and Calvinism.
