Recognition and Accreditation

1. Quartz Hill School of Theology has been granted candidate status with the Accrediting Commission International.
2. Our units have been accepted for transfer by several other colleges and seminaries. Nevertheless, before taking classes with us, if you are planning on transferring to another institution, please check with them first to make sure they will accept our units.
3. The Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI) has approved Quartz Hill School of Theology to grant Continuing Education Units (CEU's) in the area of Bible.
4. Quartz Hill School of Theology is a member of the ACL Christian Library Consortium.
5. The Library Director is a member of the Association of Christian Librarians.
6. Faculty are members of academic associations such as the American Academy of Religion and the Society of Biblical Literature.
7. One faculty member is listed in Who's Who in Religion, 1992-93, Who's Who in America, and Who's Who in the World.
