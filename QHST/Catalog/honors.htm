Honors at Graduation

       To receive honors at graduation, a student must:

1. Complete a minimum of 24 units of work in letter-graded courses at this institution.
2. Earn a GPA of 3.5 or better in all work taken at this institution.
3. Earn the following in all undergraduate courses, including transfer work:

Summa Cum Laude: GPA of 3.90 or better
Magna Cum Laude: GPA of 3.75 to 3.89
Cum Laude: GPA of 3.50 to 3.74
