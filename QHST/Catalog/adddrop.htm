Add/Drop Procedure

       Classes may not be added after the first week of instruction.
       Classes may be dropped during the first week, with no record of it appearing on the student's transcript.
       Classes dropped after the first week of instruction will be recorded as WP or WF, depending on the student's grade at the time the class is dropped.
       All classes dropped during the last two weeks of the quarter will receive an F grade, except in extreme circumstances.
