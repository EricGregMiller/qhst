Admission Requirements

One Year Diploma Program

       A high school diploma is recommended but not required for entrance into the One Year Diploma Program.

Associate or Bachelor Degree Program

       Applicants desiring entrance into an Associate's or Bachelor's program must first graduate from high school or complete its equivalent. Students actively attempting to complete their high school equivalency may take classes but will not be admitted to the Associate or Bachelor programs until they get their high school diploma or its equivalent.

Master's Degree Program

       Applicants desiring entrance into the Master's program must first complete a Bachelor's program or its equivalent. See the Graduate Division section of the catalog for more details.
