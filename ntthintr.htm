B311 New Testament Theology

3 Units

5 CEUs

1. Course Objectives: The objective of this course is to provide the student with a basic understanding of New Testament Theology, and to build a foundation for further study of New Testament Theology

2. Course Requirements: The student will need to read the lectures included here as well as the assigned readings from the textbook. During the course of the study, the student will be required to submit assignments; and at the end of the course the student will have to submit an examination.

3. Course Outline:

a) New Testament Theology or New Testament Theologies?

b) The Theology of Jesus

1- According to Mark
2- According to Matthew
3- According to Luke
4- According to John

c) The Theology of Paul

d) The Theology of the Post Pauline Church

1- Hebrews
2- Peter
3- James

4. Textbooks: The student will need to read assigned passages from the Bible, as well as segments of the textbook, G.B. Caird, New Testament Theology. Oxford: Oxford University Press. Paperback.

Books may be purchased through The Campus Bookstore.
