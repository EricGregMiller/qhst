Response to Anti-Semitism

Don't be confused. What we have attempted to do here is respond to the unbiblical and unhistorical attacks leveled by those who will try to misuse the Bible and history to deny that the Jewish people are descended from Abraham, Isaac and Jacob, or who try to argue the racist doctrine that the white, Anglo-Saxons are the true Israelites.
