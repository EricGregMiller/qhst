The Book of Leviticus

I. Title

The English title comes from the Greek translation of the Old Testament (the Septuagint). In Hebrew, the book's title is taken from the first word, in the book vayiqra-- which means "and he called".

II. Author

Moses is traditionally assumed to be the author of the book of Leviticus. Please see the discussion at the beginning of Genesis for more information.

III. An Outline of Leviticus

I. The Law of Sacrifice 1:1-7:38
II. The Consecration of the Priests 8:1-10:20
III. The Clean and the Unclean 11:1-15:33
IV. The Holiness Code 16:1-26:46
V. Dedications 27:1-3
