Mt. Baden-Powell Hike Information

Click here to see the pictures from our hike.

The hike is from Vincent Gap (6593ft) to Mt. Baden-Powell (9399ft) [Latitude and longitude 34.35866N, 117.76474W]. The hike is an 8 mile round trip. Parking requires a National Forest Adventure Pass. This info is from Dan's Hiking Pages.

The San Gabriel River Ranger's office (626 335-1251) is where to find info. They are listed on the Forest Service contact info page. Adventure passes can be purchased at several locations in Wrightwood. In the Angeles, Cleveland, Los Padres and San Bernardino National Forests there are now (as of Nov 10, 2006) three Adventure Pass free days: National Public Trails Day (June), National Public Lands Day (September) and Veterans Day (November).
