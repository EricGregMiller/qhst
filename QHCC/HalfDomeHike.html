Half Dome Hike

Half Dome

Men from the church hiked up Half Dome in July 2007. Information about that hike is here.

We are planning to hike up Half Dome again in 2008. This year the camp and hike are open to all, not just men. We will camp at Bridalveil Campground July 8 through July 12. Even if you don't wish to climb Half Dome you can come. You can come, go on our short walks, go to Yosemite Valley or just hang around camp as you wish.

Trip costs include gasoline, park entry permits, campsite fees and food. The trip will cost about $70 - $75 per person (a break down of estimated costs is here). The cost may vary especially if gas prices change dramatically. Please also plan to bring money for lunch on the drives up and back, shopping in Yosemite Valley and a couple of meals in Yosemite Valley.

Here is our tentative camp schedule.

  • Tuesday July 8: Drive in and set up camp. Afternoon walk up Sentinel Dome.
  • Wednesday July 9: Morning walk to Fissures and Taft point. Afternoon Yosemite Valley. Leave cars(s) at trail end.
  • Thursday July 10: Climb Half Dome.
  • Friday July 11: Sleep in. Sunset and stars at Glacier Point.
  • Saturday July 12: Drive home.

Practice Hikes

Here is the practice hike schedule.

  • Saturday, February 2, 8am: Old Ridge Route.
  • Saturday, March 1, 8am: Vasquez Rocks.
  • Saturday, April 5, 6am: Devil's Punchbowl.
  • Saturday, May 17, 7am: Bouquet Canyon.
  • Saturday, June 7: Mt. Baden-Powell.
On each hike you should plan on bringing plenty of water and some food. Usually we stop for lunch at the top or halfway point and trail snacks often help keep your energy up. Although the main purpose of the practice hikes is to prepare for Half Dome, anyone who is interested in a time of hiking and fellowship is invited.
