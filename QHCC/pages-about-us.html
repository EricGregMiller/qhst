About Us

Our church is not a building. It is people empowered by the Holy Spirit to share Christ with as many as possible. It is a worshipping fellowship, experiencing an awareness of God, recognizing his person, and responding in obedience to his leadership. It helps our community and our world in Jesus' name. It is Christlike in its daily living. As Paul wrote:

Just as a body, though one, has many parts, but all its many parts form one body, so it is with Christ.  (1 Corinthians 12:12 NIV)

  • For people who don't fit in other churches.

  • Family oriented.

  • Informal.

  • Grounded in Biblical Christianity.

  • Haven for those hurt elsewhere.

  • Science friendly.

  • Southern Baptist.

Service Times

Sunday School 10AM to 10:50AM
In everything that we do at church, we hope to tell people about Jesus' work on the cross so that they can become Christians. Once a person has become a Christian, then the church, in its various activities, helps him or her learn how to live his or her life as a worker for Jesus Christ. Sunday School is an important element in helping give this instruction. Available for every age group from nursery through the oldest adult, the classes are designed to allow plenty of opportunity for asking questions and discussion.

Sunday Worship Service 11AM to Noon
The contemporary worship and Bible-centered teaching is designed to guide the seeker to a knowledge of Christ and the believer to a closer walk with Him

Sunday Evening Bible Study 5:30PM to 6:30PM
Sunday evening Bible Study is a time for closer fellowship and directed teaching. The setting is informal and the teaching style is interactive with a heavy emphasis on group discussion.

Wednesday Dinner & Prayer Time 6:30PM to 8:30PM
Wednesday evening services offer a dinner before the time of prayer begins. Prayer is informal and in small groups. There is also plenty of time to visit and fellowship.

Service Content

Preaching
The sermon is biblically-based, designed to reach people where they are, and to impact them in a practical way.

Music
An integral part of the Sunday Morning service is the worship led by the praise band.  The music is varied in style and includes the newest contemporary songs to the oldest hymns.

Nursery
Nursery is provided at all services. It is designed to create a foundation for learning abut the Bible and important social skills for infants and toddlers. It includes reading of short Bible stories, singing, interactive play, and the usual care as necessary. "We will not all sleep, but we will all be changed." (1 Corinthians 15:51b)

Children's Church
During the sermon, age-appropriate instruction is provided for children in a small-group setting.
