Current Status July 2011

July giving was again decent at $6,863 for a five-Sunday month. Our effective balance improved slightly which makes eight straight months of improvement. Furthermore, for the first time in over a year we have no unpaid bills at the end of a month. This makes the effective and General Fund balances the same, at $1,312. It's great to see a positive trend continue for this long and continue into summer. To see the complete report for 2011 or previous years follow one of the links below.
