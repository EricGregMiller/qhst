December 2006 Report

Overview

The increase of our general fund to $4,326 looks like good news, and it is, but not as good as it appears. December was a five Sunday month and all income is reported (there are no outstanding offerings). Furthermore, December is usually an up month, followed by down. Finally, the December building payment of $1,137 was not made until January (treasurer mistake). In light of this, a more accurate balance would be $3,189. We basically broke even, which is not such a bad thing. All bills are paid and up-to-date.

A refrigerator was bought for the parsonage, which reduces the parsonage fund and the main account balance. The temporary fund balance of $139 is due to two things: $224 received for Lottie Moon, but not sent in December; and $85 spent on the youth calendar fund raiser, but none of the funds raised have been deposited yet.
