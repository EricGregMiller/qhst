The Finances at Quartz Hill Community Church

Status for January 2019

After lots of year-end giving in December, our January giving fell back to a more usual $3,234. The General Fund balance fell slightly to $27,484. We spent $518 for permit fees and $4,600 for engineer studies which is reflected in the Renovation Fund balance dropping to $14,139.
