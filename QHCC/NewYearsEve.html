New Year's Eve

The Millers are again hosting a New Year's Eve party. The party starts when you show up on New Year's Eve, usually around 7pm. Come early and celebrate East Coast New Year at 9pm. Come late and celebrate West Coast New Year at midnight. Come for the duration and do both! There will be food, games, fun and fellowship. Drinks and some snacks will be provided. Feel free to bring some food, even a Christmas left over, to share. The party lasts until a little after midnight.
