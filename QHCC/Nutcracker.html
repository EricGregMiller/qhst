Nutcracker Performance

Cerro Coso Community College is putting on a performance of the Nutcracker which features our own Alex and Tabitha Newman and Haley McCully. In addition, Dave Reynolds guest stars as the Rat King. Performances will be 8pm Saturday, December 2 and 2pm Sunday, December 3 at the Mojave High School gym. Admission is $7 for adults and $5 for children and can be purchased at the door. You can contact Kathy or Marla for tickets or with any questions.
