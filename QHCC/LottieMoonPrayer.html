Lottie Moon Prayer Week

This week is set aside to pray for the Lottie Moon Christmas Offering. Each evening during the week we will have an activity and prayer time to support this effort.

    Here are the events for the week. Each will start at 7pm.
  • Sunday, Dec 3: Concert of Prayer. Pastor Don will lead a moving service that guides individuals in a formal prayer time.
  • Monday, Dec 4: Mail. Bring Christmas card for the church family. Stocking will be up and you can purchase "postage" for the cards as a way of donating to the Lottie Moon Offering.
  • Tuesday, Dec 5: Shannon Murray Donations. Shannon Murray spoke to our church about a year ago and his wife has since passed away from cancer. We will raise money to purchase a gift and card for him and his family.
  • Wednesday, Dec 6: Sweet Hour of Prayer.
  • Thursday, Dec 7: Abundant Sunday Donations. Bring your donations for our monthly Grace Resources Abundant Sundays. This month we are collecting canned soup, chili, crqackers and can openers.
  • Friday, Dec 8: Pizza and Prayer. We will order pizza and pray while waiting for it to be delivered.
  • Saturdayday, Dec 9: Movie Night. Bring popcorn and your favorite beverage to watch "The End of the Spear."
  • Sunday, Dec 10: Communion Service. This will be a regular Sunday evening communion service.
