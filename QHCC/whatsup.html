What Are We Up To?

Sunday School Sunday School meets every Sunday morning at 10:00. In everything that we do at church, we hope to tell people about Jesus' work on the cross so that they can become Christians. Once a person has become a Christian, then the church, in its various activities, helps him or her learn how to live his or her life as a worker for Jesus Christ. Sunday School is an important element in helping give this instruction. Available for every age group from nursery through the oldest adult, the classes are designed to allow plenty of opportunity for asking questions and discussion.

Worship Services The teaching in the evening and Wednesday services are Bible-centered, designed to instruct believers so that they learn how to live as Christians. The Sunday morning services are especially directed to reach those who have not yet become Christians.

Music Included in all worship services, music is provided by piano and other instruments; the music is varied and upbeat, ranging from the newest contemporary songs to the oldest hymns. Occasionally there are solos and other specials.

Youth The youth group meets every Wednesday evening for their own time of Bible Study and prayer. They periodically arrange outings, sleep-overs, and parties (with adult supervision).

Children In addition to the Sunday School program, we have the Kids 4 Christ Club for ages 4-12 on Wednesday nights; it is a time of fun activities that teach about the Bible, helping children discover how to live as Christians. It also provides the opportunity for those who are not yet Christians to make that decision.

Nursery Nursery is provided at all services and is designed to be more than just a baby-sitting service. We hope to provide a foundation, so that when the babies become older they will want to become Christians.
