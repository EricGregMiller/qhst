Half Dome Hike 2007

We Did It!

We hiked Half Dome! We left Wednesday, July 11, and returned Saturday, July 14. Wednesday afternoon we took a practice hike to Sentinel Dome and went to Glacier Point at night to view stars. Thursday we took a practice hike to Taft Point and visited Yosemite Valley. Friday we hiked Half Dome. Pictures and videos are here. For fun you can check out Dan Vs Wild. Most of Eric and Dan's pictures are here and here. They offer more details and a chronology of our trip but they comprise a lot of photos.

Background Information

To encourage himself to get into shape, Don has set a goal to climb Half Dome this summer. He is inviting any interested me to join him. The Half Dome climb is not easy and those interested should train regularly, especially those of us who were born at a more comfortable distance from the apocalypse and haven't done much physical activity in a while. To help encourage regular training we are having practice hikes about once a month.

Here is the practice hike schedule.

  • Saturday, October 14 Godde Hill.
  • Saturday, November 11 Mt. Baden-Powell.
  • Saturday, December 9 Bouquet Canyon.
  • Saturday, January 13 Devil's Punchbowl.
  • Saturday, February 24 Vasquez Rocks.
  • Saturday, April 14 Willow Springs.
  • Sunday, May 20 Lake Hughes.
On each hike you should plan on bringing plenty of water and some food. Usually we stop for lunch at the top or halfway point and trail snacks often help keep your energy up. Although the main purpose of the practice hikes is to prepare for Half Dome, any man who is interested in a time of hiking and fellowship is invited.
