Continuing Education Units

       CEU's can be used to fulfill both initial and renewal Bible requirements for Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI) Certification. In order to obtain an initial Standard teacher or administrator certificate, the applicant must have 9 quarter units or 18 CEU's of Bible. Once the applicant has obtained a Standard or Professional certificate, CEU's can be used for meeting the Bible requirement for renewal. Five CEU's of Bible (or 3 quarter units) are needed to renew either of these certificates.
       The courses listed here are six hour seminars, normally conducted in the course of a single day (or sometimes two consecutive evenings). They do not count toward college units and may be used only toward fulfilling the Continuing Education Unit requirements of the Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI).
