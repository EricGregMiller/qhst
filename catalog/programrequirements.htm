Graduate Division

Program Requirements

       To enter the Master's program a student must have completed a Bachelor's Degree or its equivalent. It is recommended that the student have an undergraduate degree in Bible, with a strong emphasis on Hebrew, Aramaic, and Greek. To graduate with a Master's the student must have a total of at least three years of Hebrew and Greek and one year of Aramaic.
       Students who transfer to Quartz Hill School of Theology without this language background will be required to make up the deficit by taking the necessary Hebrew, Greek and Aramaic classes.
