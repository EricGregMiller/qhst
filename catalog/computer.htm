9. Computer Science

       The courses in computer science are designed to give the theological student a general, practical introduction to the use of computers.

C101 Typing
3 units

       An introductory course that teaches the student how to type. Directed study with exercises on a computer.

C201 Introduction to Computing
3 units

       (Prerequisite - an ability to type) An introduction to the computer, with instruction in the use of the Internet, Windows, word processors, and Bible programs. The students will also learn what to look for when purchasing a computer.
