Recognition and Accreditation

1. Our units have been accepted for transfer by several other colleges and seminaries. Nevertheless, before taking classes with us, if you are planning on transferring to another institution, please check with them first to make sure they will accept our units.
2. Faculty are members of academic associations such as the American Academy of Religion and the Society of Biblical Literature.
3. One faculty member is listed in Who's Who in Religion, 1992-93, Who's Who in America, and Who's Who in the World.
