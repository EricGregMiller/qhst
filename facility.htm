Facilities

       The Quartz Hill School of Theology is located at 43543 51st Street West (on the corner of Avenue K and 51st Street West) in the facilities of Quartz Hill Community Church.

1. Library: The QHST library is housed in Quartz Hill Community Church and contains more than 7200 volumes, in addition to audio tapes, video tapes, filmstrips, slides, and periodicals. New books are constantly being added.
2. Classrooms: Classrooms are located in the facilities of Quartz Hill Community Church.
3. Student housing: QHST does not own or provide dormitories or other living quarters for students or their families. Each student is responsible for finding his or her own living quarters.
4. Food service: QHST does not provide food services.
