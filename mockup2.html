Welcome to Quartz Hill School of Theology

We offer a free and open educational resource for self-learners around the world. Our mission is to advance knowledge and education, and serve the world in the 21st century. Quartz Hill School of Theology offers courses on a wide range of subjects. We offer the classes as shareware, which means that you don't have to pay unless you want to. As with other shareware, there are some advantages to paying. For instance, you'll get academic credit for your work. Check out a course, try it on for size, see if it fits. .
