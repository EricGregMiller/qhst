B478 Revelation I: Introduction

Purpose: To introduce the student to the book of Revelation.

Course Requirements: To accomplish the above mentioned goal the student will be required to read the book of Revelation.

Time Requirements: B478 Revelation I is designed to be completed in one academic quarter (10 weeks).

Textbooks:

The student must have a Bible (preferably in a modern translation).

The student should also select a commentary on the book of Revelation.

Books may be purchased through The Campus Bookstore.
